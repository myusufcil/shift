Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Summary: Shift respects your privacy. Your habit data stays on your device. We only collect minimal data necessary to provide our services.

1. Information We Collect

Shift collects minimal information to provide you with the best habit tracking experience:

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and display name.
  • Habit Data: Your habits, completions, and progress are stored locally on your device and optionally synced to our secure servers if you enable cloud backup.
  • Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Shift app
  • Sync your data across devices (if enabled)
  • Send you notifications and reminders (if enabled)
  • Improve our services and user experience

3. Data Storage & Security

Your habit data is primarily stored locally on your device. If you choose to create an account and enable sync, your data is securely stored using Firebase, which employs industry-standard encryption and security measures.

4. Third-Party Services

Shift may use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase: For authentication and cloud storage
  • RevenueCat: For managing subscriptions
  • Google/Apple Sign-In: For convenient authentication

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your habit data
  • Opt-out of analytics collection

6. Children's Privacy

Shift is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
